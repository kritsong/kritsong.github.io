</tr>
<tr class="even">
<td style="text-align: right;"></td>
<td style="text-align: left;">Thesis: “Facial Analysis with Deep
Learning and Auxiliary Spatial Features from Facial Landmarks”.
Supervised by Dr. Stefanos Zafeiriou. My research focuses on
discriminative and generative models for face analysis with deep
learning. In particular, I incorporated the feature extraction power
from Deep Convolution Neural Networks (DCNNs) with spatial features
provided by facial landmarks. I developed a Deep and Deformable model
specifically designed to perform facial landmarks detection. For face
image and video generation, I deployed facial landmarks as guiding
target labels, which enable more delicate and flexible face editing.
Lastly, I proposed a DCNN for face forensic localization that utilizes
transfer learning and multi-task learning.</td>
