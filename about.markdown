Asst. Prof. Dr. Kritaphat Songsri-in is a Computing Lecturer at Nakhon Si Thammarat Rajabhat University, where he teaches Machine Learning and Deep Learning. His research focuses on applying Machine Learning to broader datasets, utilizing statistical priors associated with each dataset.

He holds a Ph.D. in Computing Research from Imperial College London, where his research focused on discriminative and generative models for face analysis with deep learning. He also holds an MEng in Computing from Imperial College London.
